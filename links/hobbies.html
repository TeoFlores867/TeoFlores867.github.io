Fishing

In the summer, I often go fishing. I've fished for about 6 years, mostly going to the nearby river. I often go with my dad but occasionally I go with a friend. Sometimes with my dad we even go further out to places in Wisconsin. I fish for bluegill and other small pan fish but I've recently started fishing for bass.
