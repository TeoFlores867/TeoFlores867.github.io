Sports

Wrestling

This year I started wrestling for the first time. I started it in the pre-season program called BTS (Beat The Streets). The program helped prepare me for when I joined Lane Tech's wrestling team. I enjoyed wrestling a lot and I plan on doing for the rest of my highschool career. In the end wrestling taught me discipline and hard work.

Basketball

In middle school I played for my school's basketball team. I started in 5th and continued to play to 8th grade. It was not quite as challenging as wrestling but still was not easy when I first started. Even to this day I hang out with friends and play basketball at any nearby basketball courts. Overall it helped me learn the basics of being on a team and what it is like to be in a sport.
